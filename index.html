About

I specialize in data science, focusing on harnessing data to solve business problems.

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a Data Science graduate student at the University of Houston and a visiting graduate at Rice University. Presently, I am working as a Data Processing Analytics and Data Visualization Specialist in the manufacturing industry, skillfully utilizing tools like SQL, PowerBI, and PHP to analyze and present Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).

Throughout my academic experience, I have gained valuable skills in supervised and unsupervised machine learning algorithms, computer vision models for advanced image processing and object detection, and NLP techniques involving transformer models and text mining. Additionally, I have experience in fine-tuning Large Language Models (LLMs). This has led to contributions to over three publications, underscoring my dedication to advancing data science.

With a solid academic background from two prestigious institutions and practical experience from my internship and more than 3 years of hands-on experience, as I approach my graduation in May 2024, I am actively seeking full-time roles where I can apply and expand my expertise.

Currently Learning: I am actively expanding my skill set in data engineering and cloud services. This includes gaining proficiency in technologies such as Apache Spark, AWS, and other cloud platforms, enhancing my ability to manage and process large-scale data efficiently.
