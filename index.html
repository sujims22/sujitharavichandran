About

During the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic, I discovered my deep passion for data science. The uncertainty and data-driven decision-making that defined this period sparked my curiosity. As the world grappled with complex problems, I found myself drawn to the potential of data to drive solutions. With a background in programming and a computer science minor, I bring a solid foundation in computational skills to my data science journey. This experience led me to shift my career path from Materials Science to Data Science.

I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Data Science at the University of Houston and have gained practical experience as a Visiting Graduate student at Rice University. My goal is to leverage the power of data for meaningful solutions. Looking ahead, I'm excited to take my passion to the next level. I'm actively seeking Data Science internships for Spring 2024 and open to full-time roles in positions such as Data Analyst, Machine Learning Engineer, and Data Scientist.
